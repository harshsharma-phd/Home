About Me

I am a PhD student at IIT Bombay, specializing in Quantum Information Theory. My research focuses on Quantum Thermodynamics and Error Suppression in Collective Quantum Systems.

Achievements

  • Prime Minister’s Research Fellowship (2022).

Research & Projects

  • Tensor network error-correcting codes.

Publications

  • Quantum error correction for unresolvable spin ensemble - Preprint on arXiv, 2024.

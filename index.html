About Me

I am a PhD student at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay specializing in Quantum Information Theory. My research focuses on Quantum Thermodynamics and Error Suppression in Collective Quantum Systems.

I hold an M.Sc. in Physics from IIT Palakkad, where my thesis was on enhancing the efficiency of quantum heat engines using kicked Ising systems. I also completed my B.Sc. (Hons.) in Physics at Pt. Ravishankar Shukla University.
